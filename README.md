## About This Repository

This repository contains the complete marketing, branding, and operational structure for The Way PDO, organized using the **Agency-in-a-BOX framework**. It serves as a centralized hub for:

- **Brand Guidelines** — Tone, voice, visual identity
- **Marketing Strategy** — Campaigns, content calendar, audience targeting
- **Competitive Intelligence** — Market positioning, competitor analysis
- **Website & SEO** — Page structure, keywords, optimization strategy
- **Content & Campaigns** — Email, social media, advertising
- **Forms & Documentation** — Parent forms, policies, handbook
- **Analytics & Performance** — ROI tracking, campaign metrics
- **Design Assets** — Logos, photography, design files

## Best Practices

### 1. Keep Everything Updated
- Update quarterly as strategies evolve
- Document campaign results in real-time
- Archive completed initiatives (don't delete)

### 2. Use Consistent Naming
- All files use kebab-case (like-this)
- Dates in YYYY-MM-DD format
- Clear, descriptive file names

### 3. Cross-Reference Files
- Link related documents for easy navigation
- Update brand voice when company messaging changes
- Keep ICP updated as you learn about ideal parents

### 4. Document Decisions
- Always explain the WHY behind strategies
- Include reasoning for positioning choices
- Record what worked and what didn't

### 5. Archive Ruthlessly
- Move old campaigns to 12-Archive/
- Keep current year in main folders
- Helps identify patterns & avoid repetition

## Marketing Workflow

### Monthly
1. Review KPIs from last month (11-Analytics/)
2. Update Content Calendar (06-Marketing/Content Calendar/)
3. Create/schedule social posts (06-Marketing/Social Media/)
4. Send email campaign (06-Marketing/Email Marketing/)
5. Monitor ad performance (06-Marketing/Advertising/)

### Quarterly
1. Review business goals (03-Goals/goals.md)
2. Analyze competitors (02-Competition/competitors.md)
3. Plan seasonal campaigns (VBS, Summer Camp, Fall enrollment)
4. Update content strategy based on performance
5. Report results & ROI (11-Analytics-and-Performance/)

### Annually
1. Conduct full competitive analysis
2. Revisit business goals & adjust
3. Update brand guidelines if needed
4. Plan year-long content strategy
5. Set marketing budget
